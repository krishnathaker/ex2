Cities of the British Isles

This magazine has been put together by me as an attempt to help fellow travellers gain a better understanding about what to expect in some of the cities in the British Isles. To give you some background, I am a 20-year old college student who solo-travelled around the British Isles for 20 days in Summer 2016. I picked the British Isles for my first European experience since I grew up in India, which was a large British Colony. The city I lived in, Bombay, has been tremendously influenced by English culture; it's evident in our streets, our buildings, the architecture, the modern urban planning, our train stations, even our post boxes! I always wanted to visit Britain to experience their culture first-hand, and I must say, it was a great experience! So here are five of the nine places I visited during my trip (ranked in no particular order). I hope you enjoy viewing the photos just as much as I enjoyed taking them!

Please use the following links to access a few photos and information about each city.

  • Galway - A beautiful and lively town on the western coast of Ireland.
  • Dublin - The magnificent capital of Ireland and the home to Guiness!
  • Edinburgh - Easily one of my favourite destinations in the British Isles! Very lively, very cool city.
  • Glasgow - The architecture of this city blew me away! I also found a Hogwarts lookalike here.
  • London - No trip to the British Isles can be complete without visiting this iconic capital of England.
